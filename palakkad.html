Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Palakkad

Palakkad Fort

Palakkad Fort is a historic fort with a blend of Kerala and European architecture. It's a symbol of the region's history and cultural heritage.

Malampuzha Dam

Malampuzha Dam is a picturesque dam surrounded by gardens, amusement parks, and boating facilities. It's a popular family destination.

Dhoni Waterfalls

Dhoni Waterfalls is a natural waterfall nestled in the Western Ghats. It's an ideal spot for trekking and enjoying the beauty of nature.

Silent Valley National Park

Silent Valley National Park is a biodiversity hotspot known for its pristine rainforests and unique flora and fauna. It's a paradise for nature enthusiasts.

Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary

Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area with diverse wildlife and scenic beauty. It offers wildlife safaris and eco-tourism experiences.

Meenvallam Waterfalls

Meenvallam Waterfalls is a cascading waterfall surrounded by lush greenery. It's a serene place for relaxation and picnics.

Nelliyampathy Hills

Nelliyampathy Hills is a hill station with coffee and tea plantations. It's known for its panoramic views and pleasant climate.

Jain Temple of Jainimedu

Jain Temple of Jainimedu is an ancient Jain temple with historical significance. It's an architectural marvel with intricate carvings.
