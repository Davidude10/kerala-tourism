Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Kottayam

Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a serene village known for its beautiful backwaters and bird sanctuaries. The Vembanad Lake and lush landscapes create a tranquil setting for relaxation and boating. The Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a haven for birdwatchers, with various species of migratory birds.

Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls

Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls is a picturesque waterfall nestled amidst rubber plantations and lush greenery. The cascading waters and serene surroundings make it an ideal spot for nature lovers and photographers. A visit to this tranquil place offers a refreshing escape from the city.

Illikkal Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a striking peak surrounded by mist-covered hills and valleys. The trek to the top offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats and the Idukki Reservoir. It's a favorite destination for adventure enthusiasts and trekkers.

Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple

Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple is an ancient temple known for its impressive architecture and religious significance. The temple's grand entrance and traditional murals are captivating. The annual festival, Ettumanoorappan Festival, attracts devotees and tourists.

Vaikom Mahadeva Temple

Vaikom Mahadeva Temple is a revered pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple's intricate architecture and historical significance make it a must-visit. The Vaikom Ashtami festival, celebrated here, draws a large number of devotees.

Bay Island Driftwood Museum

Bay Island Driftwood Museum showcases a unique collection of intricate sculptures crafted from driftwood. The artworks represent various themes and characters, creating an interesting and creative experience for visitors.

Marmala Waterfalls

Marmala Waterfalls is a hidden gem tucked away in the forested landscapes of Kottayam. The waterfall cascades from a height, creating a mesmerizing sight. The surrounding nature and peaceful ambiance make it an ideal spot for a peaceful retreat.
