Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Kannur

Kannur Beach

Kannur Beach is a beautiful stretch of coastline known for its pristine shores and sunset views. It's a great place for relaxation and beach activities.

St. Angelo Fort

St. Angelo Fort is a historic fort with Portuguese origins. It offers a glimpse into Kerala's colonial past and provides scenic views of the Arabian Sea.

Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach

Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach is one of the few drive-in beaches in India. Visitors can drive along the shoreline and enjoy the sea breeze.

Arakkal Museum

Arakkal Museum is a museum dedicated to the Arakkal family, the only Muslim royal family in Kerala. It houses artifacts and memorabilia of historical significance.

Kannur Fort Beach

Kannur Fort Beach is located near the St. Angelo Fort and offers a serene environment for beachgoers. It's a great spot for a leisurely stroll.

Payyambalam Beach

Payyambalam Beach is a beautiful sandy beach with well-maintained gardens. It's a popular spot for picnics and evening walks.

Dharmadam Island

Dharmadam Island is a secluded island near Kannur known for its natural beauty. It's accessible by a narrow strip of land during low tide.
