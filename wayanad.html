Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Wayanad

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area known for its rich biodiversity. It's a great place for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Chembra Peak

Chembra Peak is the highest peak in Wayanad and offers a challenging trek. It rewards trekkers with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Edakkal Caves

Edakkal Caves are natural caves with ancient rock carvings. They provide a glimpse into prehistoric civilization and offer a unique exploration experience.

Pookode Lake

Pookode Lake is a scenic freshwater lake surrounded by lush greenery. It's an excellent spot for boating and enjoying the serene ambiance.

Banasura Sagar Dam

Banasura Sagar Dam is the largest earth dam in India. It offers boating, trekking, and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

Thirunelli Temple

Thirunelli Temple is an ancient temple surrounded by lush forests and hills. It's a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus.

Kuruvadweep

Kuruvadweep is a group of small islands on the Kabini River. It's known for its pristine natural beauty and is an ideal place for a peaceful getaway.
