Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Kozhikode

Kozhikode Beach

Kozhikode Beach is a popular coastal destination known for its scenic beauty and sunset views. It's an ideal place for a leisurely stroll.

Beypore Beach

Beypore Beach is famous for its historic Beypore Fort and pristine sandy shores. It's a great spot to relax and explore maritime history.

Kappad Beach

Kappad Beach is a historically significant beach where Vasco da Gama first landed in India. It offers a peaceful environment for beach lovers.

Thusharagiri Waterfalls

Thusharagiri Waterfalls is a scenic waterfall site with trekking trails and lush greenery. It's a paradise for nature enthusiasts.

Mananchira Square

Mananchira Square is a beautiful park surrounding a man-made lake. It's a serene place to unwind and enjoy the outdoors.

Peruvannamuzhi Dam

Peruvannamuzhi Dam is a scenic dam surrounded by gardens and offering boating facilities. It's a great place for picnics and relaxation.

Pazhassiraja Museum

Pazhassiraja Museum is a historical museum that showcases artifacts and memorabilia from the region's history. It's a must-visit for history buffs.
