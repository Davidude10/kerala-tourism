Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Pathanamthitta

Gavi

Gavi is a picturesque eco-tourism spot located in the Periyar Tiger Reserve. Surrounded by lush greenery and serene landscapes, Gavi offers a perfect escape into nature. It's a haven for birdwatchers and wildlife enthusiasts, featuring a variety of flora and fauna. Activities like trekking, boating, and jeep safaris make for an adventurous experience in this tranquil paradise.

Perunthenaruvi Waterfall

Perunthenaruvi Waterfall is a stunning cascade surrounded by rocky terrains and dense forests. The waterfall offers a mesmerizing sight as the water rushes down from a considerable height, creating a misty atmosphere. It's a popular spot for picnics and nature lovers. The natural beauty and soothing sound of the waterfall make it a must-visit destination in Pathanamthitta.

Konni Elephant Training Centre

The Konni Elephant Training Centre is a unique attraction where visitors can observe and learn about the training and care of elephants. The center offers insights into the traditional methods used to train elephants, and visitors can even participate in activities like elephant rides and baths. It's an educational and memorable experience for all ages.

Sabarimala Temple

Sabarimala Temple is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites in India. Nestled in the Western Ghats, this temple is dedicated to Lord Ayyappa. The temple is known for its strict religious practices and the annual Mandala Pooja pilgrimage. The trek to Sabarimala is a spiritual journey that attracts millions of devotees every year.

Achankovil River

Achankovil River is a scenic water body known for its religious and natural significance. It is believed to be a sacred river and has several temples along its banks. The river's tranquil beauty and the surrounding greenery make it a peaceful place for relaxation and reflection.

Adavi Eco-Tourism

Adavi Eco-Tourism is an enchanting destination with lush forests, meandering streams, and thrilling activities. The eco-tourism site offers boating, trekking, and nature walks. Visitors can enjoy the beauty of the surrounding landscapes and engage in outdoor adventures.

Kaviyoor Rock Temple

Kaviyoor Rock Temple is a historical and architectural marvel, featuring intricate carvings and sculptures. This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is carved into a large rock. The temple's serene surroundings and intricate artwork make it a place of both spiritual significance and artistic beauty.
