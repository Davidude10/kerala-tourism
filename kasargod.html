Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Kasaragod

Kasaragod Beach

Kasaragod Beach is a beautiful coastal destination known for its tranquil shores and scenic views. It's an ideal spot for relaxation and beachcombing.

Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort is a historic fort with a rich heritage. It offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and is a popular attraction for history enthusiasts.

Kappil Beach

Kappil Beach is a serene and secluded beach known for its backwater estuary. It's an excellent spot for picnics and enjoying the natural beauty.

Chandragiri Fort

Chandragiri Fort is another historical fort with a picturesque location near the Chandragiri River. It offers insights into the region's past.

Valiyaparamba Backwaters

Valiyaparamba Backwaters is a network of scenic backwater stretches. It's an ideal place for boat cruises and birdwatching.

Ranipuram

Ranipuram is a hill station known for its lush greenery and trekking trails. It's a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
