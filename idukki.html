Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Idukki

Munnar

Munnar is a picturesque hill station known for its lush tea plantations, misty landscapes, and cool climate. The town offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats and is a favorite spot for honeymooners and nature enthusiasts.

Thekkady

Thekkady is famous for the Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve. It offers opportunities for wildlife watching, boat cruises on the Periyar Lake, and exploring spice plantations. The vibrant flora and fauna make it a nature lover's paradise.

Idukki Arch Dam

Idukki Arch Dam is one of the highest arch dams in Asia, nestled amidst scenic surroundings. The dam offers panoramic views of the reservoir and the hills. It's a marvel of engineering and a must-visit attraction in Idukki.

Vagamon

Vagamon is a serene hill station known for its rolling meadows, pine forests, and tea gardens. The cool climate and tranquility make it an ideal spot for relaxation, trekking, and paragliding.

Anamudi Peak

Anamudi is the highest peak in South India and offers trekking opportunities amidst breathtaking landscapes. The trek to the peak takes you through dense forests and allows you to enjoy panoramic views of the surroundings.

Kalvari Mount

Kalvari Mount is a popular religious destination with a Christian pilgrimage center. It offers stunning views of the Idukki Reservoir and the hills. The area is known for its peaceful ambiance and spiritual significance.

Thommankuthu Waterfalls

Thommankuthu Waterfalls is a series of cascading waterfalls set amidst lush greenery. It's a great spot for trekking and picnicking. The serene atmosphere and natural beauty make it a refreshing escape from city life.
