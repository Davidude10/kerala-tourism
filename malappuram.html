Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Malappuram

Malappuram Hill Station

Malappuram Hill Station is a scenic hill station known for its lush greenery and serene atmosphere. It's a great place for nature lovers and trekkers.

Kottakkunnu

Kottakkunnu is a historical park with a fort and beautifully landscaped gardens. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Adyanpara Waterfalls

Adyanpara Waterfalls is a picturesque waterfall surrounded by lush forests. It's a great spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

Pazhayangadi Mosque

Pazhayangadi Mosque is a historic mosque known for its stunning architecture and cultural importance. It's a place of worship and historical significance.

Nedumkayam Rainforest

Nedumkayam Rainforest is a pristine rainforest with rich biodiversity. It's an ideal destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is a haven for migratory birds and offers a great bird-watching experience. It's a paradise for bird enthusiasts.
