Tourist Spots and Attractive Destinations in Ernamkulam

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi is a historical neighborhood known for its colonial architecture, cultural diversity, and charming streets. It's famous for the Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, and Vasco da Gama Square. The area is a fusion of Indian, European, and Asian cultures.

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a picturesque promenade along the waterfront offering stunning views of the backwaters and the cityscape. It's a popular spot for evening walks, boat rides, and enjoying the sunset.

Mattancherry Palace

Mattancherry Palace, also known as the Dutch Palace, is a historical palace known for its exquisite murals and artifacts. It showcases a blend of Portuguese, Dutch, and Kerala architectural styles.

Wonderla Amusement Park

Wonderla is a popular amusement park offering a wide range of thrilling rides and entertainment. It's a great place for families and adventure enthusiasts to enjoy a day of fun and excitement.

Paradesi Synagogue

Paradesi Synagogue is one of the oldest synagogues in India, located in the heart of Jew Town in Kochi. It features stunning architecture and a rich history. The synagogue is known for its impressive Belgian chandeliers.

Kuzhupilly Beach

Kuzhupilly Beach is a serene and less crowded beach known for its natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere. It's a perfect spot for relaxation, beach walks, and enjoying the beautiful sunset.

Hill Palace Museum

Hill Palace Museum is the largest archaeological museum in Kerala. It showcases the royal artifacts, paintings, and exhibits from the Cochin royal family. The museum is surrounded by lush gardens.

Cherai Beach

Cherai Beach is a pristine beach with golden sands and calm waters. It's a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the coastal beauty. The beach also has a unique blend of sea and backwaters.

Bolgatty Palace

Bolgatty Palace is a heritage hotel located on Bolgatty Island. The palace offers scenic views of the backwaters and is known for its Dutch architecture. It's a perfect blend of history and luxury.

Willingdon Island

Willingdon Island is a man-made island known for its port activities and luxury hotels. It's a hub of commercial and tourist activities, offering a glimpse into the city's maritime culture.

Kodanad Elephant Training Centre

Kodanad Elephant Training Centre is a facility for the training and care of elephants. Visitors can watch elephants being bathed and trained. It's a unique opportunity to get close to these majestic creatures.
